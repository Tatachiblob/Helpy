Helpy Now

Clients who wishes to avail services from HelpyNow are paired with a supplier which is referred to as "Helpy Partners". Tasks that are requested must only range walking distance of the partners or .03km. Requests that ask partners to go beyond can be considered out of bounds. Compensations for these services must start at a base rate of 50 pesos that can scale up depending on request. Helpy partners can choose not to accept these jobs when the compensations are too low or job request seems impossible. Environmental or surroundings can change the prices such as rain, floods, and foot traffic and time of day. Once compensation amounts have been confirmed there cannot be any change of prices by any party until the job is finished. If there are unprecedented events that require the job to canceled or discontinued, then only a 40% of the money will be transferred to the partner for their effort and time served. When a job is finished money is only released when both the client and partner confirms through their own accounts the job is finished.

Helpy Rent

Rented items are available for service starting at 9 am to 7pm. An item will only be considered reserved when there is a payment deposited to the system for the item. Items can be rented in an interval of 24 hours. When the item is not returned on the appointed time it will be considered rented for the next 24 hours. However, there will be a grace period of 15 mins from the exact time on the system. Request for items to be put aside or reserved before payments will not be entertained by the administration. After payment has been made, customers can give a time slot they wish to pick up the item. Items values are shown to customers on the site before they avail of the item. By availing of the item it is under duly diligence of the client to take care of the item. If anything becomes broken or lost during the time it is borrowed, the item's displayed value must be played by the client to the system. In the case of failure to pay due amount to the helpy website the admin put their accounts on hold till the due balance is paid. Failure to pay will result in filing the collection of the due amount through legal means using the terms and conditions as legal binding. Before handed off to the customer the customer and admin on site will inspect the item for effectiveness and functionality of the item. Anything that can decrease the item's value such as smoothness, color etc. will be shown before handing of the item. When the item will be returned there will be another inspection of the item to see if the item is return in the condition it was given.
